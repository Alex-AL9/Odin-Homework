Sandwich

About the Sandwich Recipe

This is a recipe to make a ham and cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 ham slices
  • cheese
  • onions
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes

Steps to Make the Sandwich

  1. Put 1 slice of bread first
  2. Add lettuce spread out
  3. Add tomatoes spread out on top of the lettuce
  4. Add onions spread out on top of the tomatoes
  5. Add cheese on top of the tomatoes
  6. Evenly spread the 3 ham slices on top of the cheese
  7. Finally, put the last bread slice on top
